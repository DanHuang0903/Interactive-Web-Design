Data Visualization

A simple question: could you pick the largest number among 50 numbers by only one glance? Well, I don't believe so. However, everyone can tell the highest bar in a bar chart in 0.1 second. Shape and color is the most intuitive approach to convey infomation for humanbeings. In other words, data visualization is a sort of interactive web tool which provides a lot of information straightforwardly.


In this section, I made up three datasets, and illustarte those datasets through different data visualization methods.
